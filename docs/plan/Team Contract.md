# **Team \#12 Contract**

## **1\. Team Vision and Goals**

Our team aims to:

* Develop the ability to collaborate effectively with multiple people on a long-term project.

* Strengthen our skills in system design.

* Gain experience working within one-week Agile sprints and learn to balance workload accordingly.

## **2\. Expectations for Meetings**

* **Weekly Meetings:**  
   Held every Monday during scheduled class time when class is not in session, or immediately after class when it is. Additional ad hoc meetings may be scheduled on Wednesdays.

* **Meeting Structure:**

  * Begin with a debrief of the previous week. Each member should be prepared to discuss completed work and reflect on how the week went.

  * Identify required tasks first on an individual basis (based on last week’s progress), then distribute remaining tasks aligned with weekly milestones.

  * Assign tasks to each member during the meeting.

* **Attendance:**  
   Attendance is expected and has been consistent. If a member cannot attend, they are responsible for reviewing the meeting minutes on GitHub.

* **Participation:**  
   All members are expected to contribute during the debrief. Beyond this, participation levels may vary based on individual working styles and personalities.

## **3\. Expectations for Communication and Collaboration**

### **Communication Frequency**

* Direct questions addressed to a team member on Discord should be answered within **24 hours**.

* No message or inquiry should remain unanswered for more than 24 hours.

### **Communication Conduct**

* Keep conversations within the appropriate Discord channels.

* Maintain professionalism and respect in all communications.

### **Communication Platforms**

* Discord

* GitHub

### **Collaboration Process**

* Pull Requests (PRs) must be opened **no later than Thursday**, with reviews completed within **two days**.

* All PR reviews must be finalized by **Saturday**.

* Personal weekly logs must be completed by **Sunday at 7:00 PM**.

* The designated team member for the week is responsible for submitting the team log, ensuring all PRs are merged, and confirming that issues are closed on time.


## **4\. Expectations for Work Distribution and Delivery**

* Project tasks are maintained in the GitHub Project Backlog.

* Tasks are selected and assigned during Monday meetings, with each member able to express preferences.

* Assigned tasks must be documented in the meeting minutes.

* Issues are created and assigned at the beginning of each week to provide clarity on responsibilities.

* Work that does not meet the team's quality standards will not be merged; changes will be requested through the PR.

* All members take shared ownership of the Capstone project, with a commitment to producing work that advances team progress each week.


## **5\. Resolution Strategy**

### **5.1 When an Issue Occurs for the First Time**

**a. Documentation:**

* All issues are documented through meeting minutes and Discord communication.

**b. Responsible Party:**

* The impacted team member documents the issue; Maddy will record it in the official minutes.

**c. Expected Change:**

* The team will discuss the issue openly, ensuring all parties are heard.

* A mutually agreed-upon compromise will be established.

* Those involved will implement the agreed solution during the following week.

### **5.2 When an Issue Occurs Repeatedly**

**a. Documentation:**

* Recurring issues will continue to be recorded in meeting minutes and reflected in peer evaluations.

**b. Responsible Party:**

* The impacted team member documents the issue; Maddy records it in the minutes.

**c. Expected Change:**

* As above, all parties will discuss the issue and agree to a compromise that should be followed in the subsequent week.

**d. Escalation and Firing Clause:**  
 The team will consider removal of a member if:

* The issue is severe and slows the overall team’s progress.

* The team member is unwilling to implement agreed-upon changes while others continue to follow through.

* Communication becomes sparse, and the team member is difficult to reach.

* A minimum of **four votes** is required to remove a team member.
